        About

        Savannah Jia is a painter and draftsperson currently studying fine arts at Citrus College in Glendora, California. Her practice spans oil and acrylic painting, works on paper, and sculpture, moving between figuration and the intimate language of everyday objects.

        She is currently pursuing transfer to a four-year fine arts program.
